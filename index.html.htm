About CLT Café

CLT Café is a cozy and welcoming coffee shop where friends gather, students study, and memories are made. It is a space designed to feel warm, comfortable, and relaxing.

We serve freshly brewed coffee, specialty drinks, and delicious pastries prepared with care. Every visit to CLT Café is meant to feel like a break from busy life — a place where conversations flow as smoothly as our coffee.

This café represents friendship, bonding, and meaningful moments shared over a simple cup of coffee.
